Our Story

We work with premium soy blends, cotton-core wicks, and fragrance compositions that evolve as they burn. From first light to last flicker, the experience is the product.

How We Make It

Made by hand.
Every single one.

01
Fragrance Blending
Each scent is developed and tested in small batches until the balance feels exactly right — not just at first, but through every stage of the burn.
02
Wax Preparation
We use a premium soy blend, melted at precise temperatures and combined with fragrance oils at the optimal moment for maximum scent retention.
03
Hand Pouring
Each candle is poured by hand into its vessel, with the wick centred and secured. The wax sets slowly at room temperature for a clean, even surface.
04
Cure & Package
Every candle cures for a minimum of 72 hours before packaging. Each box is assembled by hand — ribbon, tissue, card, and seal — before leaving our studio.
